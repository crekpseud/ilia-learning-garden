# Heideggerian Path Thinking

Status: untrusted seed
Created: 2026-06-25
Related experiment: [[learning/gardening/gravity-map]]

## Seed

The gravity-map experiment became more grounded when the user connected it to forest bathing: entering a forest, listening inwardly, and moving in the direction one feels pulled.

That recalled a possible Heideggerian theme: thinking as being underway on paths rather than executing a predetermined route. Relevant nearby notions include:

- `Holzwege`: forest paths or wood paths, often translated through *Off the Beaten Track*
- `Feldweggespräch`: conversation on a country path about thinking
- `Gelassenheit`: releasement or letting-be
- `Lichtung`: clearing, where something can show itself
- `Unterwegs`: being on the way

## Possible Garden Meaning

The garden may not only store concepts. It may disclose paths when the gardener attends to what is opening.

This reframes the Gravity Map:

- not a priority ranking
- not a productivity dashboard
- not a cosmic ontology
- a noticing practice for where attention wants to walk
