# Candidate Concepts

## Persistent Wiki

A Markdown knowledge layer that accumulates maintained synthesis over time instead of requiring each query to rediscover knowledge from raw sources.

Decision: not promoted yet. Likely reusable, but should wait until there are multiple packets that need this concept.

## Schema-Guided Wiki Maintenance

The practice of giving an LLM explicit conventions and workflows for maintaining a Markdown wiki.

Decision: not promoted yet. This may become a project concept after schema work begins.

## Wiki Linting

Periodic health checking of a knowledge wiki for contradictions, stale claims, missing links, orphan notes, and uncreated concepts.

Decision: not promoted yet. Keep as a future operation candidate.

## Compounding Knowledge Artifact

A knowledge artifact that becomes more valuable as sources, questions, answers, and maintenance passes accumulate.

Decision: not promoted yet. Useful phrase, but may be too abstract for a first concept note.
