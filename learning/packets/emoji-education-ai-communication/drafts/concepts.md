# Candidate Concepts

## Digital Symbol Literacy

- Status: new candidate
- Definition: The ability to interpret and use pictorial digital symbols, such as emojis, to navigate tone, social cues, cultural context, and emotional nuance in digital environments.
- Why it matters: Modern communication increasingly depends on visual symbols that are not fully captured by plain verbal literacy.
- Possible aliases: Emoji Literacy, Visual Vernacular, Digital Semiotic Literacy
- Related existing notes: [[wiki/concepts/digital-garden]], [[wiki/concepts/knowledge-gardener]], [[wiki/concepts/playful-knowledge-practice]]
- Promotion recommendation: maybe, after human contact
- If existing, suggested patch: N/A

## LLM Emoji Interpretation

- Status: new candidate
- Definition: The capacity and failure modes of language models when interpreting emoji meaning from pragmatic, cultural, social, and textual context.
- Why it matters: Emoji interpretation tests whether an AI assistant can infer contextual meaning rather than defaulting to common associations.
- Possible aliases: AI Emoji Reasoning, Emoji Disambiguation, Contextual Emoji Reasoning
- Related existing notes: [[wiki/synthesis/llm-wiki-pattern]]
- Promotion recommendation: maybe, after human contact
- If existing, suggested patch: N/A

## Context-Dependent Meaning

- Status: new candidate
- Definition: The principle that the meaning of a sign, symbol, phrase, or object is determined by its surrounding situation, including culture, timing, relationship, domain, and social intent.
- Why it matters: It may become a cross-cutting concept that links emoji interpretation, software metaphor, note titles, and AI-assisted reading.
- Possible aliases: Pragmatic Context, Semantic Disambiguation, Situated Meaning
- Related existing notes: [[wiki/synthesis/digital-garden-ethos]], [[wiki/concepts/software-as-theater-metaphor]], [[wiki/concepts/note-titles-as-apis]]
- Promotion recommendation: maybe, but only if the user sees value beyond the emoji packet
- If existing, suggested patch: N/A

## The Pharmakon of Digital Expression

- Status: future candidate
- Definition: A framing where digital symbols act as both remedy and poison: they can enrich expression and social connection, but can also flatten emotion, automate response, or intensify social pressure.
- Why it matters: This may be a good synthesis lens for the tension between playful expression and ritualized or performative digital behavior.
- Possible aliases: Digital Expression Pharmakon, Emoji Pharmakon
- Related existing notes: [[wiki/concepts/playful-knowledge-practice]], [[wiki/concepts/the-ritualization-trap]], [[wiki/concepts/the-stream]]
- Promotion recommendation: not yet
