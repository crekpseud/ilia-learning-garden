## Notebook Sources

- Title: Evergreen Notes
  Author: Jorge Arango
  URL: https://jarango.com/2023/02/02/evergreen-notes/
  Description: An introduction to the philosophy of evergreen notes, contrasting them with transient notes and highlighting their role as external expressions of evolving mental models.

- Title: Evergreen Notes: A Note-Taking System
  Author: Prisal (Scribd upload)
  URL: unknown
  Description: A summary of Andy Matuschak's system, emphasizing notes as a tool for thinking and the implementation of reading/writing inboxes.

- Title: Andy Matuschak's Working Notes (Collection)
  Author: Andy Matuschak
  URL: https://notes.andymatuschak.org/Evergreen_notes
  Description: The primary source for the evergreen note philosophy, covering principles of atomicity, concept-orientation, dense linking, and the generation effect.

- Title: How to Take Smart Notes Book Summary
  Author: Sonke Ahrens (Shortform Summary)
  URL: unknown
  Description: A summary of the Zettelkasten-based Smart Notes method, which serves as a foundational precursor to the evergreen notes system.

- Title: Permanent vs. Evergreen notes: Am I thinking about this correctly?
  Author: r/Zettelkasten Community
  URL: unknown
  Description: A community discussion exploring differences between permanent notes in a traditional Zettelkasten and evergreen notes that evolve over time.

- Title: Taking Notes (or why I do not use your favorite text editor)
  Author: Xavier Llora
  URL: https://xavierllora.net/content/2021/05/taking_notes.pdf
  Description: A presentation on the evolution of note-taking from Memex to modern digital tools, focusing on intentional learning journeys.

- Title: Tending Evergreen Notes in Roam Research
  Author: Maggie Appleton
  URL: https://maggieappleton.com/evergreens
  Description: A practical walkthrough of managing an evergreen system within a Digital Garden context using Roam Research.
