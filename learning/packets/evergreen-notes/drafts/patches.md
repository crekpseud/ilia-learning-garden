# Suggested Patches

## Patch: [[wiki/concepts/digital-garden]]

Rationale:
Evergreen notes give the digital garden concept a candidate note-level practice: ideas grow through atomic, concept-oriented, revisable notes written in the gardener's own words.

Suggested addition:

```markdown
Evergreen note practice may be one way a digital garden grows at the note level: individual ideas are written in the gardener's own words, kept small enough to recombine, and revisited as understanding changes. The practice is useful when it deepens thinking, but it should not turn tending into optimization labor.
```

## Patch: [[wiki/synthesis/digital-garden-ethos]]

Rationale:
The packet links the generation effect to the garden's tending metaphor.

Suggested addition:

```markdown
The garden metaphor becomes more concrete through evergreen note practice. Writing notes in one's own words is not merely capture; it is a form of tending that strengthens memory and understanding through the generation effect.
```
