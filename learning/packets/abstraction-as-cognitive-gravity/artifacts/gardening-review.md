# Gardening Review

## Packet

- Packet: [[learning/packets/abstraction-as-cognitive-gravity/packet]]
- Operation: `garden.review`

## Whole-Garden Meaning

This packet is less about one metaphor than about a recognizable learning signature. The user repeatedly starts with formal software details and moves toward questions of meaning, ontology, and architecture.

That pattern strongly reinforces [[wiki/synthesis/original-thought-as-active-reconstruction]]. The user is not outsourcing understanding to ChatGPT; they are using the conversation as friction for rebuilding meaning.

## Unexpected Links

- [[wiki/concepts/object-thinking]] becomes newly relevant because the packet asks how abstractions become centers of behavior and responsibility.
- [[wiki/synthesis/object-thinking-and-the-digital-garden]] already provides a bridge between software metaphor and knowledge-garden metaphor.
- [[wiki/concepts/knowledge-gardener]] is reinforced by the packet's need for a map of curiosity before promotion.
- [[wiki/concepts/playful-knowledge-practice]] matters because metaphors like gravity and mycelium keep exploration lively without making it shallow.
- [[wiki/concepts/note-titles-as-apis]] may connect later because good abstractions and good note titles both become stable handles for future thought.

## Emerging Narrative

A larger narrative is forming:

```text
the user's learning often follows a route from mechanism to meaning
meaning becomes a reusable abstraction
abstractions become attractors for future learning and agent work
the garden preserves those attractors as Markdown
```

This is a strong personal lens for the garden. It may explain why Object Thinking, digital gardens, agentic skills, and Markdown protocols all feel related rather than separate.

## Protocol Pressure

The packet validates [[docs/adr/0030-use-maps-of-curiosity-as-optional-sense-making-artifacts]]. Long AI conversations need a way to preserve trails before synthesis.

It also suggests a future protocol distinction:

- source-heavy packet import
- AI conversation capture
- curiosity-map-first normalization

That distinction is not urgent yet. One real normalized packet is not enough evidence for a new operation.

## Directional Risk

The main risk is metaphor intoxication: the gravity framing feels powerful, but it may start explaining everything too easily. The packet should eventually be tested against concrete examples from software history and the user's own work.

Another risk is over-note-making. Long conversations can become endlessly harvestable. The curiosity map should help choose the next path, not invite exhaustive extraction.

- Treat `Entropy-Abstraction Cycle` as the supporting dynamic pattern.
- Defer promotion of `Cognitive Entropy` until the term is tested against learning science or information theory.
- Revisit `Skill As First-Class Software Artifact` after the pending agentic AI packet receives human contact.
- Use the curiosity map when the user returns to decide which trail deserves the next packet.

## What Remains Unresolved

The packet still needs the user's deeper touch. The current reflection selects the framing, but it does not yet decide whether the trusted artifact should be a synthesis note, a concept note, or a broader map of the user's architectural worldview.
