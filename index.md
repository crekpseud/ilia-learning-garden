### Concepts

- [[wiki/concepts/digital-garden|Digital Garden]] - Personal knowledge space where notes are linked, evolving, contextual, and owned by the author.
- [[wiki/concepts/the-stream|The Stream]] - Timeline-first information architecture that serializes relationships into chronological feeds.
- [[wiki/concepts/evergreen-note|Evergreen Note]] - Reusable, revisable note that accumulates insight over time and can be recombined with other notes.
- [[wiki/concepts/the-ritualization-trap|The Ritualization Trap]] - Failure mode where a knowledge practice becomes self-optimizing maintenance instead of learning.
- [[wiki/concepts/note-titles-as-apis|Note Titles as APIs]] - Naming practice where note titles become stable handles for humans, links, and agents.
- [[wiki/concepts/first-principles-reasoning|First-Principles Reasoning]] - Reasoning practice that breaks problems into fundamental truths before rebuilding understanding or solutions.
- [[wiki/concepts/desirable-difficulties|Desirable Difficulties]] - Learning friction that slows short-term performance but improves retention, retrieval, and transfer.
- [[wiki/concepts/object-thinking|Object Thinking]] - Software-design lens that models systems as communities of collaborating behavioral objects.
- [[wiki/concepts/software-as-theater-metaphor|Software-as-Theater Metaphor]] - Object Thinking metaphor that separates actors, roles, scripts, and stage.
- [[wiki/concepts/self-evaluating-rule|Self-Evaluating Rule]] - Rule modeled as a first-class behavioral object rather than controller logic.
- [[wiki/concepts/option-maybe-pattern|Option/Maybe Pattern]] - Explicit-value approach to absence or possible failure.
- [[wiki/concepts/knowledge-gardener|Knowledge Gardener]] - Person or agent who tends conditions for knowledge growth rather than imposing rigid hierarchy.
- [[wiki/concepts/playful-knowledge-practice|Playful Knowledge Practice]] - Principle that learning systems should preserve play, curiosity, and lightness.
