# Gravity Map

This is an experimental whole-garden sense-making artifact.

Use it to notice which ideas currently exert the strongest pull on the garden. It is not a ranking of importance, truth, recency, or promotion priority.

Read gravity as a pull of attention, closer to forest bathing than physics. Enter the garden, get quiet, notice what draws you, and move a little in that direction.

## How To Read Gravity

An idea has gravity when it:

- appears across multiple packets or trusted notes
- attracts new questions
- changes how older notes read
- creates next learning paths
- carries strong human excitement or correction
- connects multiple domains
- risks becoming too beautiful, broad, or ritualized
