# The Stream

## Definition

The Stream is an information architecture that collapses relationships into a single-track timeline of events.

## Why It Matters

The Stream is the dominant shape of social media, feeds, and many activity-driven tools. It is useful for immediacy and conversation, but weak for timeless research, deep synthesis, and topographical navigation.

For this vault, the Stream acts as the negative foil for [[wiki/concepts/digital-garden]]. It clarifies why trusted knowledge should be navigated through concepts, links, maps, and synthesis notes, while process history can remain chronological in `log.md` and Git.
