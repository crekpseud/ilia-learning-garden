# Keep attachments out of the trusted wiki

Attachments may live in `raw` or `learning`, but not inside the trusted `wiki` area. Trusted wiki notes can link to source attachments, learning artifacts, and records, but the wiki itself should remain a clean set of reviewed Markdown notes.

**Considered Options**

- Allow attachments anywhere in the vault.
- Keep attachments only outside the vault.
- Store attachments in `raw` or `learning` and link from trusted notes.

**Consequences**

The wiki stays easy to read, diff, search, and reuse as LLM context. Source and learning artifacts remain available through links without turning trusted knowledge folders into asset dumps.
